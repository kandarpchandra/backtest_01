What is MISSING (The "Session 5" Advanced Features)

1. Data & Universe Management Layer

UniverseManager (dynamically listing/delisting instruments, rather than hardcoding them)
ContractRollManager (handling futures back-adjustments)
MarketCalendar (exchange holiday awareness)


2. Sizing & Portfolio Construction

KellySizer (optimal bet sizing algorithm)
RiskParitySizer (balancing risk equally across all held assets)
CorrelationMonitor (preventing overexposure to highly correlated assets)


3. Advanced Execution & Microstructure

MarketMicrostructureModel (bid-ask bounce, adverse selection models)
LatencyModel (simulating network and exchange latency)
VenueAdapter (routing orders to different simulated exchanges)


4. Advanced Analytics

AttributionEngine (breaking down alpha vs beta, Brinson attribution)
BootstrapCI (confidence intervals to prove Sharpe isn't just luck)
CapacityEstimation (how much money the strategy can trade before ruining its own edge)
